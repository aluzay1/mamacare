About MamaCare Platform

Your comprehensive healthcare companion designed specifically for Sierra Leone's healthcare infrastructure

What is MamaCare?

MamaCare is a revolutionary healthcare management platform that connects patients with healthcare providers, streamlines medical record management, and enhances healthcare service delivery across Sierra Leone. Our mission is to improve healthcare accessibility and quality for every citizen.

Comprehensive Healthcare

Access to hospitals, pharmacies, and healthcare professionals all in one place. Find the care you need quickly and efficiently.

Secure & Private

Your health information is protected with state-of-the-art security measures and FHIR-compliant data standards.

Maternal Care Focus

Specialized features for pregnancy tracking, maternal care, and birth record management to support mothers and families.

Mobile Optimized

Access your healthcare information anywhere, anytime with our mobile-responsive design that works on all devices.

Digital Records

Keep all your medical records, prescriptions, and health history in one secure, easily accessible digital location.

24/7 Access

Round-the-clock access to your health information, emergency contacts, and healthcare provider directory.

How to Use MamaCare

Getting Started with Your Healthcare Journey

  1. 1

    Get Registered by Healthcare Provider

    When you visit a healthcare professional, they will register you in the system and provide you with a secure 6-digit PIN for accessing your records.

  2. 2

    Complete Your Profile

    Fill in your complete medical history, including allergies, current medications, emergency contacts, and pregnancy information if applicable.

  3. 3

    Find Healthcare Providers

    Browse our directory of verified hospitals, pharmacies, and healthcare professionals. Filter by location, specialty, or services needed.

  4. 4

    Access Your Records

    Use your PIN to securely access your medical records, view your health history, and manage your healthcare information.

  5. 5

    Stay Connected

    Keep your information updated, track your health progress, and maintain communication with your healthcare providers.
